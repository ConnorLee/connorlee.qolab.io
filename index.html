What is QoLab?

Beyond the traditional confines of CoWorking, QoLab breathes imagination and discovery back into CoWorking Spaces with our immersive and whimsical space; designed for Queer Artists, Entrepreneurs, and Innovators. Our Labs offer members 24/7 access to our open WorQspace, Private Solo and Multiplayer Labs, Meeting and Exhibitor Space, fully-equipped Qraft Lab, and immersive Potion Bar at the heart of QoLab. Our goal is simple actually - to provide our members with a inspiring WorQspace to craft, cultivate and showcase their skills to help create a platform for change.

Hacking ProduQtivity

Over the years, the Workforce Paradigm has shifted and it is more important than ever to stay productive and connected. We connect our members on a virtual and physical level, giving members access to a network of artists, entrepreneurs, freelancers, and creative strangers of all kinds. Our immersive design takes gaming and entertainment to a new level of productive by kickstarting your imagination of infinite Qolaborations.

All the tools. All the time.

WorQspace isn’t only for laptops, some of us like to get our hands dirty to create. Our WorQspace is designed for multifunctional use, and for the really messy projects, the Qraft Lab has you covered - literally. QoLab gives its members access to all the tools and supplies you may need for your next project! So, whether you are a Cosplayer, Web Designer, Programmer, Painter or Comic Book Illustrator - we got you covered.

Our Cosmic Mission

It is our Mission to use our Core Imagination to tap into the ever evolving world of gaming and pop culture to provide an inspiring, collaborative and productive workspace, for individuals or entities whom share our mission to Level Up Human Rights.

Core Imagination

Gaming - We are Gamers - regardless of our profession. We do more than work. We play, unafraid of inspiration and new ideas, we use gaming to face life’s obstacles, not run from them. Productivity - We are Productive. We utilize our passions in life to kickstart our imagination, unafraid to share our ideas. We connect productive players to productive teams.
